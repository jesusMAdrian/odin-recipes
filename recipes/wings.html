Crispy Wings

Zingy Chicken Wings

Your Only concern will be whether you made enough because these Wings will be FLYING off the dinner table (pun intended).

Ingredients

  • Fresh or thawed chicken wings
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Unsalted butter
  • Garlic powder
  • Hot sauce of choice(e.g. Franks Original Red Hot, Lousiana, Buffalo, etc.)
  • Baking powder

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cut wings in half to seperate drums from wingettes. Preheat oven to 400Â°, pat the wings/wingettes dry using paper towel, cover a rimmed baking sheet with foil and place a wire rack over the baking sheet.
  2. To make the dry seasoning: combine the garlic powder, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Toss wings in dry seasoning until fully coated. Place wings onto wire rack.
  4. Put wings onto center of oven for 50 minutes. Flip wings at half point.
  5. In a bowl: stur together melted butter, sugar and hot sauce.
  6. Place wings onto bowl, while tossing, drizzle sauce mixture onto wings.(add to taste)
  7. Serve with prefered sauce.(e.g. Ranch, Blue cheese, avacado ranch, etc.)
