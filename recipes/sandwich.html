Breakfast BLT Sandwich

This sandwich is not just the perfect hangover cure but a perfect way to start ANY morning!

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Rye bread
  • 3 oz. of bacon
  • 1 egg
  • 1 avacado
  • 1 Roma tomato
  • 2 green lettuce leaves
  • Chipotle mayo
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil

Cooking Instructions

  1. Put foil over a rimmed cookie sheet, place wire rack over cookie sheet, place bacon onto wire rack.
  2. Bake the bacon at 400Â° for 20 minutes.
  3. Cook eggs over medium heat in 1/2 Tbsp of olive oil. Do not flip. Place lid over pan for the last 30 seconds to fully cook egg whites.
  4. Add some butter to skillet and place bread. Toast until golden brown.
  5. Spread chipotle mayo on one side of each bread slice.
  6. Add the bacon and egg onto one slice of bread, lettuce, tomato, and sliced avacado to the other slice. Combine together in perfect harmony.
